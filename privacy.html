03
How We Use Your Information

Information we collect is used only for the following purposes:

  • Providing and improving Smart Money Scanner features
  • Processing subscription payments and sending receipts
  • Communicating important service updates or changes
  • Responding to your support requests
  • Preventing fraud and maintaining security
  • Complying with legal obligations
Your trading journal data stays in your browser's local storage. It never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
04
Third-Party Services We Use

Smart Money Scanner uses the following third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Finnhub.io — Real-time stock price data. Your ticker searches may be logged by Finnhub per their terms.
  • Massive.com — Options chain data. Data requests are subject to Massive.com's privacy policy.
  • Stripe — Payment processing. Stripe handles all credit/debit card data. We never store card numbers.
  • PayPal — Alternative payment processing. PayPal handles all PayPal transactions.
  • Netlify — Website hosting. Netlify may collect standard access logs.
  • Google Fonts — Typography. Google may receive font requests from your browser.
05
Cookies & Local Storage

Smart Money Scanner uses browser local storage (not traditional cookies) to save your preferences and trading journal data on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible by us or any third party
  • Can be cleared at any time through your browser settings
  • Includes: language preference, journal entries, challenge start date

We do not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels.
